How to play

CONTROLS

USE WASD TO MANEUVER YOUR CAR ON THE STREET. W TO DRIVE FORWARD, A AND D LEFT OR RIGHT AND S TO HIT THE BREAK AND DRIVE BACKWARDS.

PRESS SPACE WHILE PLAYING TO GET INTO THE PAUSE MEN HERE YOU CAN ADJUST THE VOLUME OR TAKE A REST BEFORE GOING ON PLAYING

POWERUPS

THE STAR POWERUP MAKES YOU IMMORTAL FOR 5 SECONDS. THAT MEANS, THAT YOU CAN DRIVE THROUGH POLICE CARS OR BARRICADES WITHOUT GETTING DAMAGED.

THE WRENCH POWERUP GIVES YOU ONE EXTRA LIFE WHEN COLLECTED. YOU CAN STACK LIVES WITHOUT A LIMIT.

THE SPEED POWERUP MAKES YOU FASTER EVERYTIME YOU COLLECT IT. IT HAST NO EXPIRATION TIME AND HOLDS ON UNTIL THE END OF A ROUND.
